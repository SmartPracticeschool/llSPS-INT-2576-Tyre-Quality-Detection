Tyre Quality Detection Using CNN

Tyre Detection


This project develops an Automatic defect detection algorithm to detect the quality of tyre which is an important and challenging issue in the tyre industrial quality control. As is well known, the production quality of tyre is directly related to the vehicle running safety and passenger security. However, it is difficult to inspect the inner structure of tyre on the surface. This algorithm classifies tyres based on their images into good and bad quality. Moreover, a series of comparative analyses demonstrate that the proposed algorithm can accurately detect the defects and outperforms other algorithms in terms of various quantitative metrics.
